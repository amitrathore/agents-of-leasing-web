Common questions

Built for operator control.

Agents of Leasing is designed around approved rules, transparent logs, human oversight, and measurable community participation.

How do you handle fair housing risk?

Operators define approved messaging, participation rules, and escalation paths. The platform should use fair housing guardrails and human oversight, but operators should still review the program with counsel.

Is this a replacement for our PMS, CRM, or ILS?

No. Agents of Leasing is a demand and attribution layer designed to work alongside existing leasing systems, marketing channels, and property operations.

Why make existing residents central?

Residents already influence renter decisions through friends, coworkers, social posts, reviews, and everyday conversations. AoL turns that real influence into managed, measurable, operator-approved organic sales.

What makes CFO Agent different from marketing analytics?

Marketing analytics usually reports channels. CFO Agent is framed around cash-flow attribution: connecting spend and influence to tours, applications, signed leases, concessions, renewals, and portfolio economics.
